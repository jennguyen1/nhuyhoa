#### Instructions

<table class = "presenttabnoh">
 <thead>
  <tr>
   <th style="text-align:left;"> instructions </th>
  </tr>
 </thead>
<tbody>
  <tr>
   <td style="text-align:left;"> Mix teriyaki sauce: ratio 2 soy sauce : 2 sake : 2 mirin : 1 sugar </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td style="text-align:left;"> Bring mixture to a boil and continuously stir on medium low </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td style="text-align:left;"> Simmer for 10-15 min or until sauce is thickened </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td style="text-align:left;"> When small bubbles start to rise/appear when mixing, pour sauce in glass jar, cool, and refridgerate for 2-3 weeks </td>
  </tr>
